    1. `generateBriefingSummary(entropyScore: EntropyScore | null, staleCount: number, missingNextActions: number, compressionCount: number, totalAtoms: number): string`
       - Use TypeScript template literals (NOT Eta.js — per research recommendation and codebase pattern).
